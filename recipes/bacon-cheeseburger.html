Bacon Cheeseburger Dip/Casserole

Ingredients

Note from Kar: this recipe originally called for a can of canned tomatoes, obviously we have to take those out but eyeball things and maybe add some cream of chicken soup or milk if there needs to be more liquid. Or add a veggie that Alex can eat that would go well. But I think it should be fine without adding anything.

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 (8oz) package cream cheese
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 cups cheddar cheese , shredded
  • 2/3 cup crumbled bacon

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. In a skillet brown ground beef, breaking apart and cooking until cooked through.
  3. In a large bowl add cream cheese, sour cream, cheddar cheese, any veggies you want to add, cooked ground beef and the bacon. Mix until well combined.
  4. Pour into a baking dish. Bake for 20-25 minutes until bubbly and hot.

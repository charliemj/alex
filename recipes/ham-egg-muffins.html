Ham and Cheese Egg Muffins

Makes 12 muffins

Ingredients

  • 1 dozen large eggs
  • 1/2 cup skim milk
  • 6 slices Candian bacon/ham diced
  • 4 slices 2% sharp cheddar diced or 4 ounces shredded
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees and spray a muffin pan with cooking spray or use a silicon pan.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together eggs and milk until light and fluffy. Stir in the Canadian bacon/ham and salt/pepper to taste.
  3. Divide egg mixture evenly between 12 muffin tins (about 1/3 cup each) then sprinkle evenly with sharp cheddar.
  4. Bake until eggs are set, 25-30 minutes. Let cool slightly to ensure eggs are set before removing from pan to serve.

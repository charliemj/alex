Oatmeal Crusted Chicken Fingers

Ingredients

  • 1 cup regular oats
  • ⅔ cup grated fresh Parmesan cheese
  • 1½ tsp Italian seasoning blend
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 pound chicken breast tenders
  • Canola oil cooking spray

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 450°. Spray a baking sheet with cooking spray or line with foil.
  2. Put the oats in a food processor and process until coarsely ground. Add cheese, seasoning blend, salt, and pepper, and pulse a couple of times to combine. Put oat mixture in a shallow bowl.
  3. Generously spray chicken tenders on both sides with canola oil cooking spray. Dredge chicken in the oat mixture. Once all the chicken has been “breaded,” spray again with cooking spray and repeat the dredging process. Place chicken on prepared baking sheet. Spray with cooking spray.
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until browned, flipping tenders over half-way through cooking time. Let sit on baking sheet for 5 minutes and transfer to cooling rack. Serve and enjoy!

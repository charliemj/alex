Beet Pizza Sauce

Makes 1 1/2 cups of pizza sauce

Ingredients

  • 2 Medium Sized Beets (or one can)
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic (can skip if you don't want to risk)
  • 1 tablespoon Olive Oil, plus more for coating beets
  • 1 tablespoon White Balsamic Vinegar
  • 1 pinch Salt

Instructions

If using canned beets, skip to the last step.

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Peel and cut beets into 2" pieces, toss with olive oil.
  3. Wrap in foil along with whole garlic cloves, place on a rimmed baking sheet Cook in oven for 40 - 50 minutes until they are soft.
  4. Remove from oven and allow to cool.
  5. Add beets, garlic (peel removed) and remaining ingredients to a food processor. Blend until smooth. Use immediately or store in a covered glass container for up to a week. If adding to pasta, you will want to add additional oil.

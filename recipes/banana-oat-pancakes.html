Banana Oat Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 large ripe banana
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 heaping tablespoon honey
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 large egg
  • butter for cooking

Instructions

  1. Place all ingredients, except egg and coconut oil in the base of a blender and blend until smooth. Add egg and pulse a few times until egg is fully incorporated.
  2. Heat a griddle or large saute pan over medium heat and melt a teaspoon or two of butter. When hot, pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides (about 2-3 minutes per side) and serve hot with maple syrup.
  3. If batter becomes too thick to pour easily, add a tablespoon or two of milk to thin.

Chicken Nuggets

Ingredients

Chicken + Oil

  • 1 pound of chicken breast, cut in chunks for nuggets
  • 2 cups (or enough to fry chicken in) canola oil

Batter

  • 1/2 cup garbanzo bean flour (chickpea flour)
  • 3 tablespoons arrowroot powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1/2 cup sparkling water

Instructions

  1. Cut chicken into nugget-sized chunks
  2. Heat the oil in a deep pot over medium high heat. Bring the oil to 350Â°F then reduce the heat on the stove to keep a constant temperature. The oil should be about 2-inches high in the pot.
  3. In a shallow and wide dish, whisk together the bean flour, arrowroot, 1/2 teaspoon salt and baking powder. Add the egg and sparkling water and stir until just combined and smooth.
  4. Dip chicken chunks in the batter. Shake off the excess batter and place them in the hot oil, working in batches of 6-8 nuggets. Cook on each side 3 to 4 minutes, until the batter has puffed up and is golden brown.
  5. Remove the nuggets and place them on a rack lined with a paper towel to cool. Continue working in batches until you have finished the meat and batter.
